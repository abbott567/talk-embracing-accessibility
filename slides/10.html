The difference between the regulations and the requirements

The Public Sector Bodies Accessibility Regulations

To meet the regulations, the following must be true:

  • meets the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 to the standard of AA (or double A)
  • has a published and compliant accessibility statement

The Government Accessibility Requirements

To meet the requirements, the following must be true:

  • meets the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 to the standard of AA (or double A)
  • has a published and compliant accessibility statement
  • has tested the srvice on a range of assistive technologies
  • has included users with disabilities in their research
